About Me

Hi, I'm Rakhi, a dedicated **UX & Media Designer** specializing in creating seamless, interactive digital experiences.

My focus is on UX design and high-fidelity prototyping, underpinned by a solid foundation in digital media management, including maintaining a WordPress site and managing multiple YouTube and Instagram channels.

I am always eager to learn, grow, and contribute to the creative process!
